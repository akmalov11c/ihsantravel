About the Umrah pilgrimage

Umrah is a beautiful spiritual journey that every Muslim makes towards Allah. Recognizes him as a messenger of peace and communicates with him, he is cleansed of impurity within.

Any pilgrim who takes this path is on the path of cleansing his soul, heart, mind and body from past sins in order to stand out from others on the Day of Judgment.
